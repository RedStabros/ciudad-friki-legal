Privacy Policy - Ciudad Friki

At Ciudad Friki, we value your privacy and comply with the Law 1581 of 2012 of Colombia.

1. Data Controller

Data processing is carried out by Mauricio Saldarriaga García (the Developer), in collaboration with Corporación Ciudad Friki (NIT: 901.597.939-0). For any questions, contact us at: udcarkangel@gmail.com.

2. Data Collection

We collect the following information to improve your experience:

  • Basic Information: Name, email, city, and profile picture.
  • App Usage: Saved events, reviews, survey responses, and preferences.
  • Activities and Gaming: Scores, story mode levels, achievements, and Frikicoins usage (loyalty points with no real monetary value).
  • Technical Information: Device model and operating system for technical support.
  • Community Content (La Taberna): Posts, replies, reports made and received in the community forum.

3. Use of Information

We use your data to:

  • Manage your account and secure authentication through Supabase.
  • Personalize event recommendations and gaming dynamics.
  • Generate anonymized and aggregated statistics about the community for cultural and academic purposes (we will never share your identifiable individual responses).
  • Operate the La Taberna community moderation system.

4. Moderation and Audit Logs

Ciudad Friki operates a community moderation system to ensure a safe and family-friendly environment. Within this framework:

  • We store moderation action records (moderation_logs) that include: the type of sanction applied, the date, the administrator who performed the action, and the registered reason.
  • These records are for exclusive internal use for administration, auditing, and appeal processing.
  • Moderation data is retained for the duration of your relationship with the platform and up to 12 additional months for audit purposes.
  • If you were subject to a sanction and wish to access the record, you may request it at: udcarkangel@gmail.com.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data to spammers or marketing companies. You authorize the use of data under these conditions:

  • Providers: Technical data shared with Supabase Inc. for storage and hosting.
  • Strategic Partners: Anonymized and aggregated survey data for census studies and cultural policy planning.
  • Benefits: Basic contact data with sponsors to offer exclusive benefits, always subject to your consent.

6. Security

We implement technical and administrative measures such as:

  • Encryption of sensitive data.
  • Restricted access protocols to the Supabase database.
  • Internal security audits.
  • Role-based access control (users, moderators, administrators).

7. User Rights

You have the right to know, update, rectify, and delete your data. You may request the deletion of your account by sending an email to udcarkangel@gmail.com or using the option within the App. We will process the deletion within a maximum of 30 days.

8. Google Authentication

When signing in with Google, we receive your name, email, and photo. We do not store your password. You can revoke this access from your Google account at any time.

9. Push Notifications

We request permission to send you alerts about events, surveys, or system updates. You can disable them from your device settings.

10. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local device storage to keep your session active and save language or theme preferences, optimizing offline performance.

11. Minors

The application requires a minimum age of 13 years. Users under 18 must have authorization from their parents or legal guardians. We do not intentionally collect data from children under 13.

12. International Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside of Colombia, including Supabase Inc. infrastructure (United States). These transfers are carried out under security guarantees equivalent to those required by Law 1581 of 2012, as authorized by the Colombian Superintendence of Industry and Commerce.
